Medication Reminders:


Offer a medication reminder feature that sends alerts to users and their companions 
when it's time to take prescribed medications.


Health Alerts and Notifications:


Allow users to set health-related alerts and notifications for doctor's appointments,
vaccination schedules, and other important healthcare events.


User Education:


Provide educational content on the importance of regular check-ups, vaccinations,
and preventive healthcare measures.


Health Tips and Resources:


Provide a library of health-related articles, videos, and resources that can educate
both elderly users and companions on various health topics.


Collaboration with Healthcare Providers:


Establish partnerships with local healthcare providers and hospitals to facilitate easy 
access to medical services when needed.
